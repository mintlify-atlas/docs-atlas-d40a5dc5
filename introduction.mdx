## What is Bazi Skill?

Bazi Skill turns Claude Code into a traditional Chinese astrology consultant. Install it once, then trigger a reading with a single phrase — the skill guides you through a structured conversation, calculates your Four Pillars chart, and delivers a comprehensive analysis of your destiny pattern.

**Four Pillars (四柱八字)** is the classical Chinese system of birth chart analysis. Your birth year, month, day, and hour each correspond to a pair of characters drawn from the Heavenly Stems (天干) and Earthly Branches (地支), producing eight characters in total — your **八字** (Eight Characters). Practitioners read the interplay of these characters to understand your temperament, major life cycles, and year-by-year fortune trends.

## What makes this skill different

Most online Eight Characters tools produce a static chart and a generic interpretation. Bazi Skill works differently:

- **Conversational and step-by-step.** The skill collects your birth details one question at a time — name, solar date, lunar date, birth hour (时辰), gender, and birthplace — so nothing is missed and nothing feels overwhelming.
- **Classical scholarship.** Every analysis references nine canonical texts. When the skill makes a claim about your day-master strength or pattern type, it cites the source.
- **Calibration loop.** After the initial reading, the skill proposes past events it expects to find in your chart and asks you to confirm them. This lets the analysis self-correct before moving on to future outlook.
- **Edge-case aware.** Birth near Solar Term boundaries, unknown birth hours, intercalary months (闰月), and posthumous readings are all handled gracefully.

## What you get from a session

A complete Bazi Skill session has three phases:

1. **Information collection** — The skill asks for name, birth dates, birth hour, gender, and birthplace, then shows a confirmation summary before proceeding.
2. **Four Pillars charting** — Year, Month, Day, and Hour pillars are calculated with Hidden Stems (藏干), Ten Gods (十神), and the full Major Luck Cycle (大运) table with onset age.
3. **Comprehensive analysis** — Day-master strength, Five-Element balance, pattern determination, current Major Luck Cycle, annual luck (流年), historical calibration, and life-area guidance covering career, relationships, and health.
