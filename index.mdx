## What you get from a reading

A complete Bazi Skill session walks through three phases:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Information collection">
    The skill asks for your name, solar and/or lunar birth date, birth hour (时辰), gender, and birthplace — one question at a time, at your pace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Four Pillars charting">
    The skill calculates your Year, Month, Day, and Hour pillars (四柱), derives Hidden Stems (藏干) and Ten Gods (十神), then lays out your Major Luck Cycles (大运) with their onset age.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Comprehensive analysis">
    You receive a structured reading covering day-master strength, Five-Element balance, pattern (格局) determination, current Major Luck Cycle, annual luck (流年), and life-area guidance for career, relationships, and health.
  </Step>
</Steps>
