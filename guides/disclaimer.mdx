## Cultural context

Bazi (八字, Four Pillars of Destiny) is a traditional Chinese metaphysical framework with a history spanning more than a thousand years. It draws on classical concepts such as Yin and Yang, the Five Elements (五行), the Heavenly Stems and Earthly Branches (天干地支), and the Ten Gods (十神).

Engaging with Bazi is engaging with a living strand of Chinese cultural heritage. Bazi Skill approaches this tradition with respect — referencing nine canonical texts and applying their frameworks faithfully. At the same time, Bazi is **not an empirical science**. Its concepts have not been validated through controlled study, and its conclusions should not be treated as objective predictions about your future.

## Scope of use

Bazi Skill is appropriate for:

- **Cultural learning** — understanding the classical Chinese view of time, destiny, and human character
- **Entertainment** — enjoying a structured reading as a reflective or social activity
- **Self-reflection** — using the framework as a prompt for thinking about patterns, tendencies, and life phases

Bazi Skill is **not** appropriate as the sole or primary basis for decisions about career changes, relationships, major purchases, medical treatment, legal matters, or any other consequential choice.

<Note>
  As a reminder at the end of every reading, Bazi Skill tells you: "命理分析仅供参考，人生在于自身的努力和选择。" (Destiny analysis is for reference only — your life is shaped by your own effort and choices.)
</Note>

## Health

If any part of a reading touches on health — constitutional weaknesses associated with certain elements, years flagged as challenging for the body, or anything else health-related — **always defer to a qualified medical professional**. A Bazi reading is not a diagnosis and cannot substitute for clinical evaluation.

## Finance

Bazi analysis may describe periods that a practitioner associates with financial opportunity or difficulty. These observations are **not investment advice**. All financial decisions must be made rationally, based on verified information, and — where appropriate — in consultation with a licensed financial adviser.

<Warning>
  Never make significant financial commitments based on a Bazi reading alone.
</Warning>

## Tone and sensitivity

Bazi Skill avoids extreme or fear-inducing statements. Phrases like "this year you will face disaster" or "your chart indicates a short life" have no place in a responsible reading. All analysis aims to be **neutral and constructive**, framing challenges as areas for attention and growth rather than fixed, unavoidable fates.

If a reading ever feels alarming or distressing, please remember that classical Bazi theory itself emphasizes human agency: the chart describes tendencies and timing, not certainties.

<Tip>
  Use the reading as a starting point for reflection, not a final verdict. The goal is insight, not anxiety.
</Tip>
