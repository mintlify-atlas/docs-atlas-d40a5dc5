Phase 2 (第二阶段：排盘计算) transforms the birth information collected in Phase 1 into a complete eight-character chart (八字命盘) and a full sequence of major luck cycles (大运). Before calculating, the skill consults its internal reference tables for five-elements, stems and branches, shichen times, and hidden stems to ensure accuracy.

## Year pillar (年柱)

The year pillar is determined by the **Lichun (立春) solar term**, not by the Lunar New Year (农历正月初一).

- Lichun typically falls on **February 3–5** of the Gregorian calendar each year.
- If you were born **before** Lichun, your year pillar belongs to the **previous** year in the 60-cycle (六十甲子).
- If you were born **after** Lichun, your year pillar belongs to the **current** year.

<Warning>
  This is one of the most common charting mistakes. A birth date of January 20 belongs to the prior year's pillar, not the current one — even though it is well into the new Gregorian year. Always verify the Lichun date for the birth year.
</Warning>

The year pillar stem and branch are read from the 60-cycle table. Yang stems (甲丙戊庚壬) pair with yang branches; yin stems (乙丁己辛癸) pair with yin branches.

## Month pillar (月柱)

Month pillar boundaries are **solar terms (节气)**, not lunar month boundaries.

Each of the twelve months is defined by its opening **jié (节)** — the first of the paired solar terms:

| Solar term | Month branch |
|---|---|
| 立春 (Lichun) | 寅 |
| 惊蛰 (Jingzhe) | 卯 |
| 清明 (Qingming) | 辰 |
| 立夏 (Lixia) | 巳 |
| 芒种 (Mangzhong) | 午 |
| 小暑 (Xiaoshu) | 未 |
| 立秋 (Liqiu) | 申 |
| 白露 (Bailu) | 酉 |
| 寒露 (Hanlu) | 戌 |
| 立冬 (Lidong) | 亥 |
| 大雪 (Daxue) | 子 |
| 小寒 (Xiaohan) | 丑 |

<Note>
  If you were born on a solar-term transition day, your chart may straddle two month pillars. The skill identifies this and presents both possibilities with brief notes on each.
</Note>

The **month stem** is derived from the year stem using the 年上起月 mnemonic:

> 甲己之年丙作首，乙庚之岁戊为头，丙辛之年寻庚上，丁壬壬寅顺水流，戊癸之年何方觅，甲寅之上好追求。

This means:

| Year stem | 寅-month stem (starting point) |
|---|---|
| 甲 or 己 | 丙 |
| 乙 or 庚 | 戊 |
| 丙 or 辛 | 庚 |
| 丁 or 壬 | 壬 |
| 戊 or 癸 | 甲 |

From the 寅-month stem, each subsequent month stem advances by one in the 10-stem cycle.

## Day pillar (日柱)

The day pillar is determined by **calendar lookup** (万年历). Because the 60-cycle advances by one each day without exception, day pillars can be computed by offset from a known reference date.

<Note>
  If you were born between **23:00 and midnight (夜子时)**, the day pillar used for the hour pillar is the **next calendar day's** pillar. This is noted in the chart output. The 00:00–01:00 window (早子时) still belongs to the current day.
</Note>

## Hour pillar (时柱)

### Determining the branch

The hour branch (地支) is read directly from the shichen table. See [Information collection — step 5](/how-it-works/information-collection#birth-hour-出生时辰) for the full mapping.

### Determining the stem — Five-Rat Escaping method (五鼠遁元)

The hour stem depends on the **day stem** and is calculated using the Five-Rat Escaping formula (五鼠遁元):

> 甲己还加甲，乙庚丙作初，丙辛从戊起，丁壬庚子居，戊癸何方发，壬子是真途。

This gives the stem of the **Zi-hour** for each day-stem group. All other hours follow sequentially from that starting point:

## Four pillars table

Once all four pillars are calculated, the chart is displayed in the following format. Ten gods (十神) are computed relative to the **day stem (日主)** as the reference point. Hidden stems (藏干) show each branch's primary qi, middle qi, and residual qi.

|  | 年柱 (Year) | 月柱 (Month) | 日柱 (Day) | 时柱 (Hour) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 天干 (Stem) | X | X | X | X |
| 地支 (Branch) | X | X | X | X |
| 十神 (Ten god) | | | — | |
| 藏干 (Hidden stems) | | | | |

- The day pillar's ten-god cell is left blank (`—`) because the day master (日主) is its own reference.
- Hidden stem weights: primary qi ≈ 60 %, middle qi ≈ 30 %, residual qi ≈ 10 %.

## Major luck cycles (大运)

### Direction rule

The direction of the luck-cycle sequence is set by the combination of birth-year polarity and gender:

| Year polarity | Gender | Direction |
|---|---|---|
| Yang year (甲丙戊庚壬) | Male | Forward (顺排) |
| Yang year | Female | Backward (逆排) |
| Yin year (乙丁己辛癸) | Male | Backward (逆排) |
| Yin year | Female | Forward (顺排) |

Mnemonic: **阳男阴女顺排，阴男阳女逆排**

### Constructing the sequence

Starting from the **month pillar**, each successive luck cycle advances the stem and branch by one position in the appropriate direction.

**Forward example** — month pillar 丙寅:

> 丁卯 → 戊辰 → 己巳 → 庚午 → 辛未 → …

**Backward example** — month pillar 丙寅:

> 乙丑 → 甲子 → 癸亥 → 壬戌 → 辛酉 → …

### Onset age calculation

The age at which the first major cycle begins (起运年龄) is calculated from the number of days between the birth date and the nearest **jié (节)** solar term:

- **Forward (顺排)**: count forward to the **next** jié.
- **Backward (逆排)**: count backward to the **previous** jié.

**Formula**: onset age = days to jié ÷ 3

The twelve jié used for this calculation are: 立春、惊蛰、清明、立夏、芒种、小暑、立秋、白露、寒露、立冬、大雪、小寒.

Remainder handling: remainder of 1 day ≈ 4 months; remainder of 2 days ≈ 8 months; no remainder = whole-year onset.

Before the first major cycle begins, the **month pillar** itself serves as the operative cycle (小运).

### Luck-cycle table format

| Cycle | Age range | Stem-branch |
|---|---|---|
| Pre-onset (小运) | 0–X years old | Month pillar |
| Cycle 1 | X–X years old | XX |
| Cycle 2 | X–X years old | XX |
| Cycle 3 | X–X years old | XX |
| … | … | … |

Each cycle covers **ten years**. The stem is generally considered to govern the first five years of the cycle; the branch governs the latter five — though some traditions hold that stem and branch co-govern the full ten years.

<Tip>
  The transition year between two major cycles (换运年) is often a period of significant change. Pay particular attention to the relationship between the incoming cycle and the original chart during these transition years.
</Tip>
