## Heavenly stem five combinations (天干五合)

| Pair 合化 | Transformation element 化 |
|-----------|--------------------------|
| 甲 + 己 | 化土 → Earth |
| 乙 + 庚 | 化金 → Metal |
| 丙 + 辛 | 化水 → Water |
| 丁 + 壬 | 化木 → Wood |
| 戊 + 癸 | 化火 → Fire |

<Info>
**Transformation condition (合化条件):** Transformation occurs only when the pair is seasonally in power (得时得令), the two stems are adjacent, and the earthly branches provide a root for the transformed element. If the condition for transformation is not met, the combination becomes a 合绊 (mutual restraint) — the two stems bind each other without transforming, reducing the strength of both.
</Info>

## Earthly branch relationships (地支关系)

### Six clashes (六冲)

子午冲 · 丑未冲 · 寅申冲 · 卯酉冲 · 辰戌冲 · 巳亥冲

### Three harmonies (三合局)

| Combination | Result |
|-------------|--------|
| 申 子 辰 | 合水局 Water frame |
| 亥 卯 未 | 合木局 Wood frame |
| 寅 午 戌 | 合火局 Fire frame |
| 巳 酉 丑 | 合金局 Metal frame |

<Note>
All three branches must be present for a full three-harmony frame. If one is missing, a half-harmony (半合) forms — for example, 申子 or 子辰 each form a half-water combination — at roughly half the strength.
</Note>

### Three meetings (三会局, 方局)

| Combination | Result |
|-------------|--------|
| 寅 卯 辰 | 会东方木局 East Wood frame |
| 巳 午 未 | 会南方火局 South Fire frame |
| 申 酉 戌 | 会西方金局 West Metal frame |
| 亥 子 丑 | 会北方水局 North Water frame |

<Note>
Three-meeting frames are stronger than three-harmony frames. All three branches must be present.
</Note>

### Six combinations (六合)

子丑合土 · 寅亥合木 · 卯戌合火 · 辰酉合金 · 巳申合水 · 午未合火（或合土，有争议 / or Earth — contested）

### Three punishments (三刑)

- 寅巳申三刑 — 无恩之刑 (ungrateful punishment)
- 丑戌未三刑 — 持势之刑 (overbearing punishment)
- 子卯相刑 — 无礼之刑 (disrespectful punishment)
- 辰辰自刑 · 午午自刑 · 酉酉自刑 · 亥亥自刑 — self-punishment

### Six harms (六害, 相穿)

子未害 · 丑午害 · 寅巳害 · 卯辰害 · 申亥害 · 酉戌害
