## Derivation rules (推导规则)

The ten gods are determined by measuring every other heavenly stem against the day master (日干 / 日主) using the five-element generation and control cycles combined with yin/yang polarity.

| Relationship 关系 | Same yin/yang 阴阳相同 | Different yin/yang 阴阳不同 |
|------------------|----------------------|---------------------------|
| Generates me (生我, my mother) | 偏印 Indirect seal (枭神) | 正印 Direct seal |
| I generate (我生, my child) | 食神 Eating god | 伤官 Hurting officer |
| Controls me (克我, my controller) | 偏官 Indirect officer (七杀 Seven killings) | 正官 Direct officer |
| I control (我克, what I overcome) | 偏财 Indirect wealth | 正财 Direct wealth |
| Same as me (同我, my peer) | 比肩 Parallel shoulder | 劫财 Rob wealth |

## Ten gods details (十神详解)

| God 十神 | Meaning 含义 | Representative people and things 代表人事 |
|---------|------------|----------------------------------------|
| 比肩 Parallel shoulder | Same element, same polarity as day master | Brothers, friends, peers, competitors |
| 劫财 Rob wealth | Same element, opposite polarity | Sisters, rivals, sources of financial loss |
| 食神 Eating god | Element day master generates, same polarity | Children (female chart), talent, good food and life |
| 伤官 Hurting officer | Element day master generates, opposite polarity | Children (female chart), artistic skill, rebelliousness |
| 偏财 Indirect wealth | Element day master controls, same polarity | Windfall wealth, father (male chart), romantic partners outside marriage |
| 正财 Direct wealth | Element day master controls, opposite polarity | Steady income, wife (male chart), stable resources |
| 偏官 Indirect officer (七杀) | Element that controls day master, same polarity | Unconventional authority, pressure, adversaries |
| 正官 Direct officer | Element that controls day master, opposite polarity | Legitimate authority, superiors, husband (female chart) |
| 偏印 Indirect seal (枭神) | Element that generates day master, same polarity | Unconventional studies, stepmother, solitude |
| 正印 Direct seal | Element that generates day master, opposite polarity | Mother, academic achievement, benefactors |

## Five-element cycles (五行生克)

**Generation cycle (相生):**

木 Wood → 火 Fire → 土 Earth → 金 Metal → 水 Water → 木 Wood

**Control cycle (相克):**

木 Wood → 土 Earth → 水 Water → 火 Fire → 金 Metal → 木 Wood

## Classical six-family mappings (六亲对应)

From 渊海子平 (*Yuanhai Ziping*), the foundational text for the four pillars system:

| God 十神 | Family member 六亲 |
|---------|------------------|
| 正印 Direct seal | Mother (母亲) |
| 偏财 Indirect wealth | Father — male chart (父，男命) |
| 正财 Direct wealth | Wife — male chart (妻，男命) |
| 正官 Direct officer | Husband — female chart (夫，女命) |
| 食神 / 伤官 Eating god / Hurting officer | Children — female chart (子女，女命) |
| 比肩 Parallel shoulder | Brothers (兄弟) |
| 劫财 Rob wealth | Sisters — male chart (姐妹，男命) |

<Note>
These mappings are the classical standard. Real chart interpretation accounts for the full eight-character structure; a single ten-god label rarely tells the whole story.
</Note>
