## Yin and yang years (阳年 vs 阴年)

Whether a birth year is yang or yin is determined by its year stem (年干):

- **Yang year (阳年):** year stem is 甲, 丙, 戊, 庚, or 壬
- **Yin year (阴年):** year stem is 乙, 丁, 己, 辛, or 癸

---

## Direction rule (顺逆规则)

| Year type 年干 | Gender 性别 | Direction 大运方向 |
|--------------|------------|------------------|
| 阳年 Yang | 男 Male | 顺排 Forward |
| 阳年 Yang | 女 Female | 逆排 Reverse |
| 阴年 Yin | 男 Male | 逆排 Reverse |
| 阴年 Yin | 女 Female | 顺排 Forward |

**Mnemonic (口诀):** 阳男阴女顺排，阴男阳女逆排  
(Yang male and yin female go forward; yin male and yang female go in reverse.)

## Sequencing method (排列方法)

Major luck cycles are sequenced from the month pillar (月柱), advancing or retreating one stem-branch position at a time.

### Forward ordering (顺排)

Starting from the month pillar, advance one position each step — stems cycle forward, branches cycle forward.

Stem forward sequence: 甲→乙→丙→丁→戊→己→庚→辛→壬→癸→甲 (循环)  
Branch forward sequence: 子→丑→寅→卯→辰→巳→午→未→申→酉→戌→亥→子 (循环)

**Example:** month pillar 丙寅, forward order → 丁卯 → 戊辰 → 己巳 → 庚午 → 辛未 → …

### Reverse ordering (逆排)

Starting from the month pillar, retreat one position each step.

Stem reverse sequence: 癸→壬→辛→庚→己→戊→丁→丙→乙→甲→癸 (循环)  
Branch reverse sequence: 亥→戌→酉→申→未→午→巳→辰→卯→寅→丑→子→亥 (循环)

**Example:** month pillar 丙寅, reverse order → 乙丑 → 甲子 → 癸亥 → 壬戌 → 辛酉 → …

## Onset age calculation (起运年龄)

The onset age is derived from the number of days between the birth date and the nearest relevant solar node (节), divided by 3.

**Formula:**

```
Onset age (years) = days to nearest 节 ÷ 3
```

- **Forward direction:** count forward from birth to the **next** solar node (节).
- **Reverse direction:** count backward from birth to the **previous** solar node (节).

### Twelve solar nodes used (十二节)

立春 · 惊蛰 · 清明 · 立夏 · 芒种 · 小暑 · 立秋 · 白露 · 寒露 · 立冬 · 大雪 · 小寒

<Info>
Each month contains one 节 (node, which begins the month) and one 气 (mid-month breath). Only the 节 is used for major luck cycle onset calculation, not the 气.
</Info>

### Remainder handling

| Remainder 余数 | Onset adjustment 起运调整 |
|--------------|--------------------------|
| Remainder 0 days | Exactly on the whole year |
| Remainder 1 day | Approximately 4 months added |
| Remainder 2 days | Approximately 8 months added |

Round to the nearest whole year for the final onset age.

## Major luck cycle period (大运周期)

Each major luck cycle governs **10 years**. Cycles run consecutively for the duration of the subject's life.

**Example** (onset age 5, month pillar 丙寅, forward order):

| Cycle 大运序 | Age range 年龄范围 | Stem-branch 干支 |
|------------|-----------------|----------------|
| 第一步 | 5–14 岁 | 丁卯 |
| 第二步 | 15–24 岁 | 戊辰 |
| 第三步 | 25–34 岁 | 己巳 |
| 第四步 | 35–44 岁 | 庚午 |
| 第五步 | 45–54 岁 | 辛未 |
| 第六步 | 55–64 岁 | 壬申 |
| 第七步 | 65–74 岁 | 癸酉 |
| 第八步 | 75–84 岁 | 甲戌 |

## Small luck before onset (交运前的小运)

Before the major luck cycle begins (i.e., before the onset age), the month pillar stem-branch itself acts as the operative luck period.

**Example:** if onset age is 5, then ages 0–4 are analyzed using the month pillar's stem and branch.

---

## Annual luck (流年)

流年 (annual luck) is the stem-branch of each individual calendar year. It operates on top of the current major luck cycle.

- Annual luck interacts with both the natal chart (四柱) and the active major luck cycle through clashes (冲), combinations (合), punishments (刑), harms (害), and other relationships.
- The major luck cycle sets the background environment; the annual luck activates specific events within it.

| Scenario | General outcome |
|----------|----------------|
| Strong major luck cycle + unfavorable annual luck | Overall stable, but the current year brings turbulence |
| Weak major luck cycle + favorable annual luck | Overall difficult, but the current year brings opportunity |

## Analysis notes (注意事项)

<Note>
The transition year when one major luck cycle ends and the next begins (换运年) tends to be a period of significant change and should receive extra attention.
</Note>

1. **天干管前五年，地支管后五年** — One school holds that the heavenly stem of a major luck cycle governs the first five years of its ten-year span, and the earthly branch governs the second five years. Another school holds that stem and branch jointly govern the full ten years. Both views exist in classical literature.
2. Analyze how the major luck cycle's stem and branch interact with the natal chart: harmonious heaven-and-earth combinations (天合地合) are generally favorable; clashing heaven-and-earth combinations (天克地冲) call for caution.
3. Always read luck cycles against the natal chart's 喜神 (favorable gods) and 忌神 (unfavorable gods): a favorable god entering during a luck cycle is auspicious; an unfavorable god entering is inauspicious.
